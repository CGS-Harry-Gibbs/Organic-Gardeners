Garden Beds

We provide a wide range of garden bed options that can range from a balcony size to as large as you'd like. Our beds will be durable and convenient, we will require one session to measure up and provide you with options. From here we are able to quickly instal your garden bed and start to plant this seasons plants for you.

Chickens

Along with garden beds, we are able to provide a chicken coop with ever-laying chickens. Our secret is every year you can have the option to change chickens and this way you will be able to have a constant supply of eggs.

Our talented gardeners can create a chicken coop in your back-garden and can provide advice on how to properly take care of your chickens. This can also be arranged in the same style as a garden bed.
